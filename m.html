How to make my animation?

Place your object in your desired place.
Tap the camera icon.
Start taking pictures while moving your object a tiny bit after each photo.
Once you are done, click on the play button.
The object seems like it moves by itself!

How to adjust my phone for stop-motion making?

You need to make your phone steady.
You can use heavy objects as camera holders.
You can use a (shoe) box or a cup next to you or a bottle.
Or get creative!

How can I change the speed of my animation?

The more pictures you have, the slower and smoother your animation will be.
